## App Overview

**ScaleKeep** is an iOS app for managing reptile care records.

### Key Features

- **Feeding Management**: Record feeding dates and automatically calculate the next feeding date. Get notified when it's time to feed
- **Growth Tracking**: View weight and length trends in graphs
- **Shedding Management**: Record from shedding signs to completion
- **UVB Light Management**: Record replacement dates and get notified when it's time to replace
- **Calendar View**: View care records in calendar format
- **Photo Album**: Save growth memories with photos
- **Home Screen Widget**: Check feeding schedules from your iPhone home screen
- **Data Export**: Export data in CSV/PDF format

### Preset Species (7 species)

The following reptiles are preset. You can also register any species as a "Custom Species":

- Ball Python
- Corn Snake
- California Kingsnake
- Leopard Gecko

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1. What iOS version is supported?

**A.** ScaleKeep supports iOS 17.0 or later.

### Q2. What can I do with the free version?

**A.** The free version allows you to register up to 3 pets. All main features including feeding records, weight/length tracking, shedding records, calendar view, and album are available for free. Upgrading to Pro gives you unlimited pet registration and removes ads.

### Q3. Can I back up my data?

**A.** Yes. Go to "Settings" → "Export Data" in the app to export your data in CSV or PDF format. You can save the exported files via email or cloud storage.

### Q4. I'm not receiving notifications

**A.** Please check the following:
1. In iPhone Settings → Notifications → ScaleKeep, make sure notifications are allowed
2. In the app's Settings, make sure "Feeding Reminders" is turned on
3. In each pet's detail screen, make sure a reminder time is set
4. Make sure Focus Mode or Do Not Disturb is not blocking notifications

### Q5. I want to register a species that's not in the presets

**A.** You can register new species from the "Custom" section in the species selection screen. You can set the species name, category (Snake/Lizard/Turtle/Other), and feeding interval.

## How to Use

### Register a Pet

1. Tap the "+" button at the top right of the home screen
2. Enter your pet's name
3. Select a species (preset or custom)
4. Enter sex, acquisition date, etc. (optional)
5. Tap "Save"

### Record a Feeding

1. Tap a pet on the home screen
2. Select the "Feeding" tab on the pet detail screen
3. Tap the "+" button at the top right
4. Enter food type, amount, and date/time
5. Tap "Save"

### Add a Widget

1. Long press on your iPhone home screen
2. Tap the "+" button at the top left
3. Search for "ScaleKeep"
4. Choose Small (1 pet) or Medium (multiple pets)
5. Tap "Add Widget"

### Set Up Notifications

1. Tap "Settings" in the app
2. Turn on "Feeding Reminders"
3. Set notification times in each pet's detail screen
