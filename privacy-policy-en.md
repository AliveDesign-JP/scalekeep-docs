# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2, 2026**

## Introduction

ScaleKeep ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ScaleKeep (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Pet Information**: Names, species, morphs, birth dates, and other details about your reptile pets
- **Care Records**: Feeding logs, weight measurements, shedding records, and notes
- **Photos**: Images you choose to attach to your pet profiles

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting purposes
- **Usage Data**: Anonymous app usage statistics to improve our services (only if you opt-in)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Store and sync your pet care data (if you enable iCloud sync)
- Send you notifications about feeding schedules and care reminders (if enabled)
- Improve and optimize our App

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

All your data is primarily stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal data unless you choose to sync with iCloud.

### iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data will be stored in your personal iCloud account. This data is protected by Apple's security measures and is only accessible by you.

### We Do Not

- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Access your data stored in iCloud
- Collect unnecessary personal information

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services

The App may use the following Apple services:

- **iCloud**: For data synchronization across your devices (optional)
- **StoreKit**: For processing in-app purchases
- **Push Notifications**: For sending care reminders (optional)

### Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics to understand how users interact with our App. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Delete your data at any time by uninstalling the App
- Disable notifications and iCloud sync in the App settings
- Request information about what data we collect

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete the App. If you use iCloud sync, your data is retained in your iCloud account until you manually delete it.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
